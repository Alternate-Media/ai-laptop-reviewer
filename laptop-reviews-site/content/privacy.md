At **Laptop Verdict**, we value your privacy as much as we value hardware performance.

## Data Collection
We do not use invasive tracking or sell your personal data. We may collect anonymous analytics to understand which "Truth Reports" are most helpful to the community.

## Cookies
We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality (e.g., saving your theme preference).

## Third-Party Links
Our reviews often link to trusted technical sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.
